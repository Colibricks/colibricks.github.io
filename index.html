How It Works

1. Launch and Control

Move your platform left and right with intuitive mouse or trackpad gestures. Launch the marble upward to begin breaking bricks.

2. Bounce and Destroy

Angle your platform to direct the marble. Realistic physics handle bounces, chaining destructions across brick formations.

3. Activate Power-Ups

Collect specials from broken bricks. Watch your marble multiply, speed up, or fire lasers to clear screens efficiently.

4. Advance and Conquer

Clear levels to unlock harder challenges. Track progress with scores and achievements tailored for macOS.
